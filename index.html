Histore of ANIMATE

1. Origins (1900s-1940s)

Manga started in Japan with picture scrolls and woodblock prints. The first Japanese animation was made in 1917. During World War II, animation was often used for propaganda.

2. Modern Anime Begins (1950s-1970s)

Osamu Tezuka, known as the “God of Manga,” created Astro Boy in 1952. In 1963, Astro Boy became Japans first TV anime series. In the 1970s, robot and science fiction anime like Gundam became popular.

3. The Golden Age (1980s-1990s)

Studios like Toei Animation and Studio Ghibli became famous. Popular works included Dragon Ball, Sailor Moon, Neon Genesis Evangelion, and Akira. Anime spread worldwide with hits like Pokémon, One Piece, and Naruto.

4. The Digital Era (2000s-Today)

Animation moved to digital production and 3D technology. Streaming services such as Netflix and Crunchyroll made anime global. Famous recent series include Demon Slayer, Attack on Titan, and Spy Family. Spirited Away won an Academy Award in 2003.

5. Cultural Impact

Anime and manga influence art, fashion, and video games. They have become a major part of Japanese culture and economy. Today, anime is enjoyed by audiences all over the world.
